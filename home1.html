About Us


The main objectives of this platform are to increase the yield of crops resulting in increased profits, optimizing irrigation and hence saving water.
The first step is to register yourself by entering your basic details. Click on the + button to add your farm. After entering all the basic details, you will be directed to module 1.

This platform is divided into 2 modules:

1) In the first module, user is expected to enter details like the soil pH, N, P, K values of his soil, Location, etc. After entering these values our system would suggest the top 3 best suitable crop which would give maximum yield in the entered conditions. The user has to Select one crop out of the three.

2) After selecting a crop, our system will display tasks to be performed on a day-to-day basis. It starts from Land Preparation to Harvesting. User has to update the system whether he has completed the task or not, according to the input the system will assign further tasks.
